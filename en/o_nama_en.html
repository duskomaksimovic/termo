About us

History

Termomontaza began its work in 1948. By going through various work and assignments, we have managed to evolve into a modern company, steadily maintain our quality and face every unsatisfied challenge. Today, our company specializes in the planning, mounting and reassembly of all types of heating, air conditioning and ventilation systems. Since its creation until today, we have realized works on many objects in the former Yugoslavia and abroad, such as in Germany, Rusland, Slovakaj, Italy, Holland, Kuwait, Alzir, ... etc., alone or in cooperation with our business partners.

Struktur Beschäftigter

Today, our company employs more than 150 highly skilled workers of various educational backgrounds who diligently carry out every job. The quality of our work comes first. In order to be able to guarantee this, our workers are continuously trained. Knowing that only in this way can we maintain the quality of our work and prove ourselves on the world market, we have brought our quality policy so far that at the beginning of 2006 we obtained the ISO 9001: 2000 certificate.
